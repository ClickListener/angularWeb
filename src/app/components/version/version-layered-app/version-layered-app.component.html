Connect App for iHealth Next

The Connect App for iHealth Next enables iHealth partner apps to easily measure vital signs using iHealth Labs' mobile health devices. Behind the scene, the Connect App leverages mobile deep links to hide the complex measurement workflow inside and achieve simple integration with our partner apps.

If you are interested in using the Connect App for iHealth Next for your vital sign measurement, please contact us at enterprise@ihealthlabs.com, with subject include "[iHealth Next Connect]".

Finally, for end users, the Connect App also provides comprehensive FAQs for iHealth devices.
